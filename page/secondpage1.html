Welcome to Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play 5v5 tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed by Riot Games, officially released in June 2020. It features precise gunplay combined with unique, character-based abilities.

Agents

Teams of "Agents" compete as attackers or defenders in a bomb-defusal mode, aiming to win 13 rounds through strategy, teamwork, and skill.

Roles

Roles describe what the agents portray in the strategic composition of the team

Initiator

initiators challenge angles through their skills and act as suppoort for the duelist

Duelist

Duelist entry the site as they cover and collect space for the team

Controller

Controllers act as also an support for the team as they close angles and spaces on the map

Sentinel

Sentinels are defense dependent on the team as they lock down sites

Gameplay

Teams of five compete as attackers or defenders to win rounds by planting/defusing the Spike or eliminating the enemy team.
